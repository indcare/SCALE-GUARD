How AMRUT Scaleguard Works

AMRUT Scaleguard uses advanced high-frequency electronic pulses to change the physical properties of calcium and magnesium minerals present in hard water. These minerals are normally responsible for scale buildup inside pipes, appliances, and fixtures.

1. Generates High-Frequency Signal

The device emits a precisely tuned high-frequency electromagnetic field through cables wrapped around your main water pipe.

2. Alters Mineral Structure

This signal changes the crystalline structure of calcium and magnesium from a hard, sticky form (calcite) into a softer, non-adhering form (aragonite).

3. Prevents Scale Formation

The transformed minerals remain suspended in the water, passing harmlessly through the system without forming scale deposits.

The process is chemical-free, maintenance-free, and preserves beneficial minerals in your water.
