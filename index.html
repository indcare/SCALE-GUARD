How AMRUT Scaleguard Works

AMRUT Scaleguard uses high-frequency electronic pulses to change the physical properties of calcium and magnesium in hard water—preventing scale without removing beneficial minerals.

1. Generates High-Frequency Signal

The device emits a tuned electromagnetic field through cables around your main water pipe.

2. Alters Mineral Structure

Converts minerals from sticky calcite to harmless aragonite.

3. Prevents Scale Formation

Minerals remain suspended, passing through without forming scale.
